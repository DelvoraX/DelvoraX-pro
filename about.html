About

Why we built DelvoraX

Most B2B sales tools were built to manage outreach, not do it. So AEs end up spending three or four hours a day on research, writing, and chasing, before a single real conversation happens.

DelvoraX was built to close that gap. One desktop app that handles the full outbound workflow, from finding the right accounts to sending the right message at the right time. The rep approves, the AI runs it.

We're still building. Early access opens soon.
